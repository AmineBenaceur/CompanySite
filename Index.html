ABOUT US



We are TRINLE!

Created in Canada, TRINLE offers coaches of individual sports a place to schedule private lessons, seamlessly create invoices, and track their athletes' progress.

No more lost papers. No more manual calculations.

Our Mission

Our vision is to ensure you can focus on your passion and athletes, not the technical difficulties of running a small business.


OUR BUSINESS


TRACK

Each athlete has their own profile where you can easily access the progress they have made with videos, notes, and to-do lists.

INVOICE

Quickly create invoices by selecting your athlete's name and the date range.

TRINLE will do the heavy lifting and pull the lesson and billing information from your schedule and the athlete's profile.


SCHEDULE

Make scheduling a breeze by setting your available hours and having all your athlete's schedules in one place.

Feel free to connect your current calendar for optimal organization!
